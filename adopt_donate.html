Donations

Here at Paws Haven we work hard to get all of our pets adopted and care for them to the best of our ability while they are with us. Any donations you are willing to make to help make these animals happy and healthy are greatly appreciated. We accept monetary donations here on our website and donations of blankets, beds, and toys on location! Thanks for your help and check out our volunteer page to get even more involved!

Next Steps

Now that you have submitted this form, the first step is done! Here is what you can expect in the next steps of your adoption journey:

  • An email confirming your application is being reviewed
  • A follow-up email letting you know if your application is accepted or denied
  • If your application is denied, you will receive an explanation
  • If your application is accepted, you will be prompted to schedule an in person meeting with out adoption staff and your prospective pet!
  • A home visit may be necessary depending on the animal you are hoping to adopt and if there are additional pets already at home.
